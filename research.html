SIREN: Technologies for Ensuring Safe and Secure Incident Response Strategies for Nuclear Facilities The future smart grid represents a significant evolution in the way electric grids function. At the core of this change is an increased use of ICT to implement enhanced monitoring and control in the distribution network at medium and low-voltage levels. Ensuring the cybersecurity and resilience of smart grids is of paramount importance. This is the target of the EU-funded SPARKS – Smart Grid Protection Against Cyber Attacks – project. 2016-Ongoing IAEA CRP J02008
